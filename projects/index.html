Problem:

Create a simple yet effective onboarding experience for a new product, ensuring that users could easily understand its features while allowing the product to adapt to their needs from the start.

Solution:

  1. Combine the account creation process with a brief survey to streamline onboarding and gather initial user preferences.
  2. Users are then guided through creating their first bot and previewing how it works, providing immediate hands-on experience.
  3. Introduce the user to a personalized bot that we've created based on their survey responses, with the option to start using it right away or create a new one at any time.
